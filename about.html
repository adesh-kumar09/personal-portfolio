My Story & Philosophy

Hello! I’m Adesh, a passionate Frontend Developer with a growing interest in web development and business. My journey began with a curiosity for digital creation, and over the past 6 months, I’ve been focused on mastering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, while also beginning to explore backend development.

I use AI tools like ChatGPT to speed up development and boost productivity, but I always refine the final code to meet my standards. Some projects in this portfolio, including the portfolio itself, were created with AI assistance, combined with my own efforts. I take full responsibility for problem-solving, learning through challenges, and continuously improving my skills.
AI is often seen as a threat to jobs, but I believe it’s a tool to enhance our work. Combined with human creativity and problem-solving, AI helps us work smarter, boost efficiency, and adapt to the fast-changing tech world.

My goal is to master development, pursue freelancing, and eventually become an entrepreneur. I'm passionate about tech, data science, and cybersecurity, and aim to build a successful business. I’m dedicated to creating responsive, visually appealing websites that deliver high-quality results and meet client expectations.
